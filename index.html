About Me

A little bit
about myself

I'm Ethan Lazar, a mechanical engineering student at Northeastern University (B.S. expected May 2028) with a minor in aerospace. Originally from Philadelphia, now based in Boston.

I'm part of AerospaceNU, where I work on liquid-propellant rocket recovery systems, and I tutor undergrads in statics, thermodynamics, mechanics of materials, and calculus through the College of Engineering. I've studied abroad with Northeastern's Global Scholars Program in London and Oakland, and I'm on the Dean's List (Fall 2025, GPA 3.78).

Outside of school, you'll find me running, on the track, cooking, or somewhere near an airplane.
